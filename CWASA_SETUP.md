## How It Works

The CWASA system uses:
- **SiGML**: XML-based sign language notation
- **JAS**: Java-based 3D avatar rendering
- **WebGL**: For real-time 3D rendering in the browser

## Avatar Models Available

1. **Marc** (default) - Male avatar
2. **Anna** - Female avatar  
3. **Luna** - Female avatar
4. **Siggi** - Male avatar
5. **Francoise** - Female avatar
